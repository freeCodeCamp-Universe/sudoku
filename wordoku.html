How to Play
Basic Rules
  • The grid: A standard 9×9 sudoku grid — fill every row, column, and 3×3 box with each of nine letters exactly once.
  • The letters: All nine letters come from a hidden word shown below the grid.
  • Same logic: Placement rules are identical to classic sudoku — just letters instead of digits.
Additional Rules
  • Find the word: Once solved, one complete row or column spells out the hidden word in order — see if you can spot it.
  • Entering letters: Use the letter buttons on screen or type the letters directly on your keyboard.
