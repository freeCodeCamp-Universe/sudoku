How to Play
Basic Rules
  • The grid: A standard 9×9 sudoku. Fill every row, column, and 3×3 box with digits 1–9.
  • White dots: A dot between two adjacent cells means those digits differ by exactly 1 (e.g. 4 and 5).
  • No dot: When two adjacent cells have no dot, their digits must not differ by exactly 1.
Additional Rules
  • Both directions matter: Dots mark consecutive pairs and missing dots rule them out. Use both pieces of information to solve the puzzle.
