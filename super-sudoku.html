How to Play
Basic Rules
  • The grid: A 16×16 board divided into sixteen 4×4 boxes. Fill every cell using digits 1–9 and letters A–G.
  • Rows and columns: Every row and column must contain all 16 symbols exactly once.
  • Boxes: Each 4×4 box must also hold all 16 symbols exactly once.
How to Play
  • Given symbols: Pre-filled cells are fixed — build your solution around them.
  • Entering symbols: Use the on-screen pad to enter digits or letters.
