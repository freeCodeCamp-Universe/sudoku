How to Play
Basic Rules
  • The layout: Four 9×9 grids are placed on a 12×12 board, overlapping at the corners to create a butterfly shape.
  • Fill with 1–9: Every cell within each 9×9 grid must contain a digit from 1 to 9.
  • Standard sudoku: Within each grid, every row, column, and 3×3 box must hold each digit exactly once.
Additional Rules
  • Shared corners: Where two grids overlap, the shared 3×3 box must satisfy the rules of both grids at the same time.
  • Solve together: The four grids are linked through their overlapping corners — none can be solved in isolation.
