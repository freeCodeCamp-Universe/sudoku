How to Play
Basic Rules
  • The grid: A 9×9 board where nine colors replace the digits 1–9. The logic is identical to classic sudoku.
  • Rows and columns: Each row and column must contain every color exactly once.
  • Boxes: Each 3×3 box must also hold every color exactly once.
How to Play
  • Filling cells: Click a color in the palette, then click a cell to paint it. Click the same color on a filled cell to erase it.
  • Given colors: Cells with a small dot are pre-filled and cannot be changed.
