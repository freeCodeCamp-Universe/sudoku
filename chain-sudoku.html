How to Play
Basic Rules
  • The grid: A standard 9×9 sudoku — fill every row, column, and 3×3 box with digits 1–9.
  • Chains: Groups of connected cells are linked by a colored chain running through the grid.
  • Chain rule: The digits within a chain must be consecutive — an unbroken sequence of numbers in any order.
Additional Rules
  • Sequence length: A chain of three cells holds three consecutive digits, such as 4, 5, and 6 — the order within the chain doesn't matter.
  • Sudoku still applies: Every chain cell must also satisfy its row, column, and box.
