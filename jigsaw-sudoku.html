How to Play
Basic Rules
  • The grid: A 9×9 board where the nine regions are irregular jigsaw shapes instead of standard 3×3 boxes.
  • Fill with 1–9: Every cell must contain a digit from 1 to 9.
  • No repeats: Each row, column, and colored jigsaw region must hold every digit exactly once.
Additional Rules
  • Region shapes: Each region contains exactly 9 cells but can be any shape — follow the color boundaries.
  • Given digits: Pre-filled cells are fixed — fill in the rest to complete the puzzle.
