How to Play
Basic Rules
  • The grid: A standard 9×9 sudoku — fill every row, column, and 3×3 box with digits 1–9.
  • Cages: Cells are grouped into dashed cages, each with a small target number in its corner.
  • Sum rule: The digits inside each cage must add up to that target exactly.
Additional Rules
  • No repeats in cages: A digit may not appear twice within the same cage, even if sudoku rules would otherwise allow it.
  • No givens: There are no pre-filled cells — the cages and their sums are your only starting clues.
